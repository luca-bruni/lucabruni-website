Welcome to lucabruni.dev!
This barebones HTML/CSS page inspired by the web of the 90s and early 2000s contains a bit about my personal life, professional experiences, and passion projects. The page is intended to be paired with a modern counterpart under the same domain (WIP). Below is a summary of each page on the navigation bar:

  • About: A bit about my personal life/interests, with some lore about my entry into the computer science/software engineering industry, and what I've been doing since.
  • Résumé: Will contain a PDF copy of my most up-to-date résumé/CV, any relevant certifications, achievements, awards, and other professional accolades.
  • Projects: Code projects from the past or ongoing (if progress is significant). Will contain descriptions, screenshots, and links to source code or project hub.
  • Games: 10/10 games. 'Nuff said.
  • 3D Prints: I recently obtained a Bambu Labs P1S + AMS. This will showcase prints, including photos, filament and hardware information, and calibration/slicing parameters. Where applicable I will also include relevant files for each model.
  • Gallery: Cool photos and videos (well, at least ones I thought were cool) that may or not be worthy to be shared on the World Wide Web - and though I promise I'm no artist by any means, a piece here and there might make the cut.
  • Software: I hope to upload or link any software I think is useful or cool, paired with system requirements or recommendations. My experiences and review of the software might be shared, along with installation instructions if necessary.
  • Links: Similar to the software page, I will give other pages the limelight here if I believe they contain some sort of useful tool or high-level entertainment value.
  • Contact: My other professional socials, emails, etc. If you spot any issues with the site, please contact me.

 

Thanks for visiting my page! Feel free to poke around and you might find something interesting.
